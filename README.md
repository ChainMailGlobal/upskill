# Upskill

**The OnlyFans of Upwork — for anyone with a skill.**

A barista, carpenter, or electrician uploads a short video of their craft. AI packages it into a professional training module. Anyone pays to be guided through it live with real-time AR handholding.

## How It Works

**Creator side:**
- Upload a video of yourself doing what you do + voice notes
- Gemini 3.1 extracts steps, timing cues, and technique details
- NanoBanana 2 generates character-consistent visual step cards
- Lyria narrates each step
- Publish on Upskill for $4.99/session

**Learner side:**
- Buy a module, open your phone camera, point it at your setup
- AR overlay shows each step floating over your workspace
- Real-time AI coaching watches what you're doing and gives feedback
- Actually learn it right

## The Problem

A barista with 10 years of craft gives that knowledge away free every shift. A carpenter's joinery technique exists only in their hands. An electrician's panel know-how retires with them.

349,000 construction worker shortage. 41% of the workforce retiring by 2031. The knowledge is walking out the door.

Upskill captures it, packages it with AI, and lets experts earn from it — forever.

## Tech Stack

- **Gemini 3.1** — long-context reasoning over videos + PDFs, live agentic vision
- **NanoBanana 2** — character-consistent visual step generation, sub-pixel text rendering
- **Lyria** — narration and audio guidance per step
- **React + Supabase** — marketplace frontend + backend
